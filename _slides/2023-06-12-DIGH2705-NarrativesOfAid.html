Unlike traditional donation marketing, Freerice is not designed to hook its user on guilt. It does not use any harsh colours, consistently prompt for monetary donations, or advertise with images of distress or struggle.

Conclusion
Both of these services follow the idea of the donor-as-consumer model, where the donor in each service is treated as a consumer. They are sold an idea of a product that is easy to use and will make a difference. While I have been quite critical of these products, I do believe they are both exceptional in rasing awareness and promoting a service that is both accessible and beneficial.
